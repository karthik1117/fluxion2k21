Welcome to Fluxion-2K21

Fluxion 2K21- A nexus of knowledge, innovation, creativity, and fun, is an Online technical fest conducted by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering of Sri Sairam Engineering College, Chennai. This is a platform for students to explore academics in a fun way. This tech fest gives space for students to participate, compete and flaunt their knowledge with students from various colleges. Come join us on the 20th of October, 2021 to experience the extravaganza.
